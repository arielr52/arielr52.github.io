Job Talk.

Free online home repair contract collaboration tool.

Designed for contractors and home owners, Job Talk provides an efficient means of communication for a home repair, home remodel, home renovation, or home improvement job.
Job talk saves all agreements, decisions, and communication between the contractor and the home owner and makes this information easily accessible online.

View on Google Play

Features

  • Text messages: Communicate with all users on a contract using Job Talk's built in messaging.
  • Online contract: Help manage work commitments and protect your home repair investments by tracking all agreements.
  • Contract templates: Select from a list of available home repair contracts.
  • Payment schedule: Track conditions for payments and when payments are made.
  • Signatures: Online signing on contract and contract change orders.
  • Change orders: Add changes to the original contract, to capture any changes in agreements.
  • Attachments: Upload receipts, images of proof of work completed prior to payment. All attachments are stored in the user's personal Google Drive.
  • Control access to contract: Select who is able to view and contribute to the contract.
  • Email: Generate contract PDF's, receive contract update notifications.
